Top 5 Summer Fashion Trends to Embrace with Ivory Streets

8th June 2024

Summer is here, and it's time to refresh your wardrobe with the latest trends. At Ivory Streets, we're dedicated to helping you stay ahead of the fashion curve. This season, we're bringing you a blend of classic styles and contemporary twists that will keep you looking chic and feeling comfortable. Here are the top five summer fashion trends you need to embrace with our latest collection.
01. Vibrant Colors and Bold Prints
This summer, it’s all about making a statement with bold prints and vibrant colors. Think tropical patterns, geometric designs, and bright hues that reflect the energy of the season. Our new collection features eye-catching pieces that are perfect for both daytime outings and evening gatherings.
(Style Tip: Pair a bold printed shirt with neutral shorts or pants to let the print shine. Add some colorful accessories to complete the look.)
02. Effortless Linen
Linen is a summer staple for its breathability and timeless appeal. Our linen collection offers a range of options, from lightweight shirts to breezy dresses, ensuring you stay cool and stylish even on the hottest days.
(Style Tip: Mix and match sustainable pieces with your existing wardrobe to create versatile and eco-conscious outfits. A simple organic cotton tee can be paired with anything from jeans to skirts.)
03. Sustainable Fashion
Sustainability continues to be a major trend, and we’re proud to offer eco-friendly options that don’t compromise on style. Our sustainable line includes pieces made from organic cotton, recycled materials, and environmentally-friendly dyes.
(Style Tip: Opt for a linen dress for a day at the beach or a casual brunch. For a more polished look, a linen blazer paired with tailored shorts can be both comfortable and chic.)
04. Athleisure Evolution
Athleisure is evolving beyond the gym and becoming a staple in everyday fashion. Our latest athleisure collection combines comfort and style, perfect for those who are always on the go. From joggers to stylish hoodies, these pieces are designed to transition seamlessly from workouts to casual outings.
(Style Tip: Layer a sleek, fitted hoodie over a sports bra and high-waisted leggings for a look that’s both functional and fashionable. Add some sporty sneakers to complete the ensemble.)
05. Minimalist Chic
Less is more with the minimalist trend that’s taking over summer fashion. Our minimalist collection features clean lines, neutral tones, and versatile pieces that can be dressed up or down. These timeless designs are perfect for creating a capsule wardrobe.
(Style Tip: A simple white blouse paired with tailored trousers can be elevated with statement jewelry and a bold lip color. For a casual look, opt for a basic tee with denim shorts and minimalist sandals.)
